## Reta Titration

Once per week (Monday AM, SubQ). Increase every 4 weeks. Stay longer at current dose if sides are rough (nausea, appetite too suppressed).

| Weeks | Dose/wk | Draw (1ml BAC recon) |
|-------|---------|----------------------|
| 1–4 | **1mg** | 5 units |
| 5–8 | **2mg** | 10 units |
| 9–12 | **4mg** | 20 units |
| 13–16 | **8mg** | 40 units |
| 17+ | **12mg** | 60 units |

## BPC/TB-500 Cycling

Two 6-week cycles with a 2-week break between. TRT/HCG/AI/Reta never stop.

| Week | BPC-157 | TB-500 |
|------|---------|--------|
| 1 | 250mcg **1x/day** (AM only) | 5mg/wk — 2.5mg Mon + Thu (load) |
| 2–4 | 250mcg **2x/day** (AM + PM) | 5mg/wk — 2.5mg Mon + Thu (load) |
| 5–6 | 250mcg 2x/day | 2.5mg/wk — **Thu only** (maint) |
| **7–8** | **OFF** | **OFF** |
| 9 | 250mcg 1x/day (AM only) | 5mg/wk — 2.5mg Mon + Thu (load) |
| 10–12 | 250mcg 2x/day | 5mg/wk — 2.5mg Mon + Thu (load) |

## TRT Base (flat — never stops)

| Compound | Dose | Schedule |
|----------|------|----------|
| Test Cyp | 160mg/wk | 80mg Mon + 80mg Thu (IM) |
| HCG (Pregnyl) | 1000IU/wk | 500IU Tue + 500IU Fri (SubQ) |
| Anastrozole | 0.5mg/wk | 0.25mg Mon + 0.25mg Thu (oral, on pin days) |
| Finasteride | 1mg/day | Daily (oral) |

Finasteride blocks test → DHT conversion, which pushes more test toward estrogen. Monitor E2 — if sides appear (water retention, nipple sensitivity), AI dose may need a bump.

## Reconstitution

Slowly down glass wall. Gentle swirl. **Never shake.** Fridge immediately. Use within 28 days.

| Vial | BAC Water | Dose | Draw | Draws/vial |
|------|-----------|------|------|------------|
| BPC-157 5mg | 2ml | 250mcg | **10 units** | 20 |
| TB-500 10mg | 2ml | 2.5mg | **50 units** | 4 |
| Reta 20mg | **1ml** | 1–12mg | **5–60 units** | See titration |
| HCG 5000IU (Pregnyl) | 2ml | 500IU | **20 units** | 10 |

**Reta uses 1ml BAC** (not 2ml) so higher doses (8–12mg) fit in a 1ml syringe. At low doses (1–2mg/wk) the vial will expire before you finish it — that's normal, just open a new one.

## Bloodwork

Pull labs 2x/year through Defy Medical. Time the draw on a **trough day** (morning before your Mon or Thu pin).

| Marker | Why |
|--------|-----|
| Total & Free Testosterone | Confirm TRT dialed in |
| Estradiol (sensitive) | Finasteride shifts more test → E2. Key marker to watch with AI dosing. |
| DHT | Should be suppressed on finasteride |
| CBC (hematocrit/RBC) | TRT raises hematocrit. Flag if >54% — donate blood or adjust dose. |
| Lipids | Reta + TRT can both affect lipids |
| Liver panel (AST/ALT) | Baseline check on NAC, anastrozole |
| PSA | Standard on TRT, especially with finasteride |

**Extra pull:** Consider one-off labs 6–8 weeks after starting Reta or changing AI dose, to confirm E2 is in range.
